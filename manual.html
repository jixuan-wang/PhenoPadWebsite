Handwritten Note Mode

Taking Notes

  • Write notes as usual, click on the pop-up phenotype to add it
  • Recognized phenotypes will show up on the top list, from which phenotypes can also be added
  • Select orange-colored words from the recognized text bar to change to other expansions of a abbreviation
  • Select words from the recognized text bar to change any recognition mistakes

Searching for Phenotype

  • Tap on a block / line of written script by finger to re-recognize handwriting and re-extract phenotypes from that line
  • Press the side button of pen to select parts of handwritings for recognition and phenotype extraction

Adding phenotypic information

  • You have to click on the Phenotype name (either from dropdown list or candidate list) to add a phenotype
  • Switch between states (Y/N) of phenotypes symptoms, Y for present, N for absence

EHR Note Editing

To use the editing mode, click on the “Edit EHR” button in the initial page. You can either import previous records by loading a .txt file or just copying and pasting into the interface.

  • To import from a .txt file: choose “from file”
  • To copy and paste: choose “from paste”
  • You can append more text by double tapping on note page and select “paste to end”

Drawing strokes on text for editing

  • Underline = select word (selected word will be changed to orange font with dashed underline). A menu will show up which allows adding a handwriting comment and searching for phenotypes based on the selected text
    • After adding handwriting comments, tap outside of comment block will shrink it to rightside, hover over to see the source of this comment
    • Remove highlighting and cancel deletion
  • Dot = insert text at clicked point, supports both handwriting / typing mode, a triangular pointer will indicate where new text will be inserted into
  • Zigzag = delete word, deleted word will be changed to gray font

Use right side panel for annotations and add-in functions as usual. You can also hover over left bottom side on “Editing Mode” for switching between editing and previewing mode. Previewing mode shows how the text will look like when exported from the note.

Speech Service

Speech service utilizes a bluetooth microphone array (raspberry pi) for voice inputs. Once user opens the note, Phenopad automatically connects to the device and an icon will show at the bottom left side once the connection is successful.

Turn on speech recording service by pressing on the microphone Icon below the bluetooth icon and wait until the icon turns blue with the status text as “ON”

During speech, any recognized Phenotypes from the transcript will pop up in the candidate bar, add or remove it as usual

Conversation Preview

  • Real-time: Click on the speech bubble button on side menu for real time speech processing
  • Past conversation history: Click on the clock button on the top menu bar to view any past conversation transcript recorded within this note
