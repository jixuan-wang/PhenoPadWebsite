Taking Notes by Hand

  • Write notes as usual, any phenotypes found will show up along with text recognition of the line.
  • Clicking on the phenotype will indicate the presence of this symptom.Clicking again will indicate the absence of the symptom.
  • All recognized phenotypes including candidates will show up on the top panel bar and allows modification by clicking.
  • Orange words indicates the predicted meaning of an abbreviation found. Click on the word to change to other possible meanings of this abbreviation.
  • In case the recognition misses the correct word, user can also manually type in the correct text
    • ⇧ Tap on a block / line of written script by finger to re-recognize handwriting and re-extract phenotypes from that line. Press the side button of the pen to select parts of handwritings for recognition and phenotype extraction
    ⇧ Hold the side button of the pen to select a part of a line for re-recognition and phenotype searching.
    Browse the left side panel to view or modify the collected phenotypic information.
    Adding phenotypic information
    • You have to click on the phenotype name (either from dropdown list or candidate list) to add a phenotype.
    • Switch between states (Y/N) of phenotypes symptoms, Y for present, N for absence.
    • The suggested phenotypes and differential diagnosis will update once phenotypic information is changed.

    ⇧ Draw a square to add drawings, images or photos. Move photos or drawings by dragging the button at top-right corner.
    ⇧ User can annnotate photo and images using differnt pen styles. Hide the control by clicking the "minimize" button at top-left corner. It will be hidden on the right side panel. Clicking on the icon again to maximize it.

    Editing on History Records

    ⇧ Import from a text file.

    Import Hisrtory records

    • To use the editing mode, click on the “Edit EHR” button in the initial page. You can either import previous records by loading a .txt file or just copying and pasting into the interface.
    • Selecting "From File" to import text from a .txt file.

    ⇧ Paste text for editing.
    ⇧ Append text to the end by pasting.

    Paste text for editing

    • Select "From Paste" if you would like to copy and paste the text into the interface.
    • To paste, first copy text from other place, then double click by finger on the text area and select "Paste to end".
    • You can append text to the end by repeating the above operations.

    ⇧ Add comments.

    Add comments

    • Use underline to select words (selected words will be changed to orange font with dashed underline).
    • A menu will show up which allows adding a handwriting comment or searching for phenotypes based on the selected text
    • Select "Annotate" to add comments.
    • After finishing, click outside the writing panel which will then be moved to the right side.

    ⇧ Insert recognized text or keep the original handwritings.

    Insertion

    • Inserting text by first clicking at the position for insertion.
    • Then write on the pop-up panel.
    • After finishing writing, click on the "+" button to insert the text.
    • If you would like to keep the original strokes, you can just click outside the panel then it will be moved to the right side.

    ⇧ Delete text by scratching out it.

    Deleteion

    • Scratching out text to delete it.

    ⇧ Highlight text.

    Highlighting

    • Hold the side button of the pen.
    • "Mark" the text you want to highlight.
    • Release the pen and the marked text will be highlighted in yellow color.

    ⇧ Cancel highlighting/deletion.

    Cancel highlighting/deletion

    • Highlighting and deletion can be canceled by first clicking on the highlighted/deleted text.
    • Then in the pop-up menu, select "Cancel Highlight" or "Cancel Delete".

    ⇧ Add comments on highlighted text.

    Add comments on highlighted text.

    • You can click on the highlighted text and add comments directly.

    ⇧ Hovering on the stickers to show the original editing position.

    Show editing position

    • Hover on the "stickers" on the right panel to show the original position where the editing takes place.

    ⇧ Use the right side to take free-form notes.

    Free-form notes

    • You can use the right panel to take free-form notes, take photos, add drawings and images. It is the same with the note taking interface.
